## AI Usage



I have used AI in a variety of different ways during this writing process. I have successfully used it for:

+ Feedback on if the right tone.
    + Abstract and Intoduction. 
+ Help with LaTex.
    + The Maculay2 scripts references and imports were generated by copilot. 
    + In the beginning, I used it for compilation errors, but it made it more difficult so I stopped. 
    + When adding references and labels to various theorems / sections.
+ Grammer and spell check.

I have used AI unsucessfully for help with the Macualay2 language. I tried it in the beginning, but its hallucinations proved disorienting and unhelpful, so I quickly turned to the documentation. Especially surrounding syntax.
